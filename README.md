# Connect

**A calmer way to stay in touch with the people who matter to you.**

Connect is your personal network-building assistant — it helps you nurture relationships
instead of letting them fade. It reads your phone's contacts and call history
(read-only, on-device) and gently nudges you toward the people you've gone quiet with.
No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Everything stays on your phone.

Connect helps you:

- **Rediscover** people you've lost touch with
- **Organize** your network into meaningful groups
- **Get reminders** to reconnect with friends, relatives, mentors, customers, and colleagues
- **Build stronger relationships** over time instead of letting them fade away

---

## Features

- **Reconnect Today** — surfaces people you're overdue to call, drawn from your call
  history. Includes a "Missed Connections" lane for calls you haven't returned.
- **Smart groups** — organize contacts into Friends, Family, Office, and Helpers.
  Create groups by hand, or let the optional assistant propose groupings for you.
- **Want to Connect** — a hand-picked "keep close" list that gets its own spotlight,
  sorted by who's most overdue.
- **Say Hi Today** — one gentle, randomized suggestion per group.
- **Milestones & streaks** — earn achievements like reconnecting with 25 people or
  holding a 7-day streak.
- **Per-contact detail** — call directly, view call history, add private notes, set
  connection goals, and track your last call.
- **Data portability** — export and import everything as portable JSON. Your data, your
  control.
- **Playful, calm design** — a "who should I call?" spinner, spotlight cards, and a warm
  palette that makes staying in touch feel inviting rather than like a chore.

## Privacy

Privacy is the foundation, not a feature.

- **100% on-device.** Contacts and call logs are read-only — Connect never edits,
  deletes, or uploads them. There are no accounts and no backend.
- **No tracking.** No analytics, crash reporting, ads, or third-party trackers.
- **You stay in control.** The optional smart grouping sends only contact *names* plus
  the profile context you choose to provide (schools, colleges, workplaces). It never
  sends phone numbers, call logs, or notes.
